About Me

My name is Robert. Most people call me Rob or Bob. I am a licensed Professional Engineer (PE) in California, currently at Degenkolb Engineers, where I work on seismic retrofits, new hospital design, computational tool development, data visualization, and various R&D initiatives.

I grew up in Toronto and I am Canadian. In 2018, I moved to the U.S to pursue my Master's degree in Structural Engineering at Stanford University. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Ottawa.

My interests include structural engineering, programming, and design. In my spare time, I like to play tennis, rock climb, run, hike, and write/code on the internet. I am the creator of several popular structural engineering python packages. Most of which are free, open-source, and available on my Github. Welcome to my little corner of the internet. Please reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate.
